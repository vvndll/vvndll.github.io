DALAKET FINE ARTS

DALAKET FINE ARTS is a fine art studio located in Batangas. All products are made by hand by local artists around the region. Items are made in small batches causing variations in form and finish from piece to piece. Variations may include but are not limited to color, texture, and size. Studio hours are 10am – 4pm Monday – Thursday.
